Riddler's Picture Show

The Chemi evil prankster is back again, this time with pictures! Summon your inner Batman to solve the Riddler's riddles and go on to decipher an explosive word at the end!!

Event type:  Technical
Participation: Team of 2-3, Chemical department
Platform: Zoom
No. of Rounds: 2
Date/Time: Jan 22nd, 11am to 12pm


Round 1
- Participants will receive the zoom link well in advance before the round starts to their respective Mail ID.
- Participants should join the zoom platform with the video ON and audio OFF.
- The Participants will be separated into teams and each team will be sent into separate breakout rooms.
- There will be 3 volunteers who will be there to see if anyone is engaged in any kind of foul play.
- For this round, a Google Form will be shared in the zoom chat section. It consists of 10 questions, which needs to be answered in 15 minutes.
- The Team should find out the common word connecting the two pictures given and give the correct chemical engineering technical word.
- Participants need to submit the Google form within 15 minutes after which it won’t accept responses.
- There should be only one submission per team (i.e.) only one Google form must be submitted per team.
- Each correct answer fetches you 10 points and no negative marking. There will also be special starred questions within the 10 questions which will be considered if there is a tie. 
- HAPPY CONNECTING 
- The top 'n' teams (depending upon how many teams register) with the highest score will be shortlisted for the final round.
- Zoom link for the shortlisted teams will be shared to their registered Mail ID. 

(Duration: 25min)


Round 2
Shortlisted teams must join the zoom with the video ON and audio OFF.
- Volunteers will be present to see if they are indulging in any kind of malpractices.
- Each Team will be given with separate breakout rooms.
- Through screen sharing, the questions will be shared by event coordinators.
- Each question will consist of five clues with which the participants must try to find the apt chemical engineering word.
- Participants can send their answers through private message on zoom chat.
- The team which answers the first only will be given points.
- There will be a total of 5 questions with 5 clues for each question.
- Each clue will be displayed for a duration of 15 seconds.
- The Team which finds the word in 1st clue will be given 50 points, if found in 2nd clue will be given 40 points, in 3rd clue with 30 points and so on.
- There will be negative marking of -10 points where if the team attempts to answer the question but fails to do so in the 1st clue.
- If the same team answers that question in the 4th, 3rd or lower clues (the points would be for ex: 30-10=20 points)
- Winner will be selected based on which team has the highest of points at the end of five questions.
- If there is a tie there will be a tie breaker question. 

(Duration: 20min)
