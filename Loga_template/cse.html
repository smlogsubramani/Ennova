Hackers' Asylum

Hackers' Asylum

Event type: Technical (No Prior Knowledge Required)
Participation: Teams of 2, Any department
Platform: TBD
No. of Rounds: 2
Date/Time: Jan 22nd, 9am to 1pm


Event Description:
Round1: Online Open Book MCQ based on Cybersecurity (20m)
Round 2: Hacking Competition (3hrs)
This is a CTF(Capture the flag) style Cyber Security event. Theme of the event is that a medical research lab had successfully found a cure for cancer. But just before the formula was published, a doctor on the research team stole the formula and disappeared after clearing out all files on the research computer. The lab has hired the participant to investigate the computer and recover the formula.The participants have to uncover clues, keys and other “flags” to uncover the puzzles one byone. Each puzzle is a security puzzle which includes OSINT, password cracking, Brute Forcing etc.
Participants will receive points for each puzzle they crack. The participants do not need anyprior knowledge except basic computer and browsing skills. They can ask for help anytime but points will be calculated accordingly. The aim of this event is to create an interest in the cyber security domain.

(1 hour qualifying round + 3 hours Final Hacking round)

Fun Oasis

1) Pictionary 2) Crime solving 3) Picture story telling 4) Kahoot Trivia

Event type: Non Technical
Participation: Individual, Any department
Platform: Google Meet
No. of Rounds: 4
Date/Time: Jan 22nd, 1pm to 4pm


Round 1
Pictionary- The participants are split into teams of 2 and one person from each team draws and the other team mate finds. The top 5 or 7 teams (yet to be decided) qualify for the next round.

Round 3
Picture story telling- In this round, the participants play individually and individual scores add up to team scores. The scores are calculated based on various factors like cohesiveness, coherence etc.A series of 5 random pictures are displayed. Participants are required to co-relate the 5 images and say a fun, quirky story.Note: This is not an elimination round.



Round 2
 Crime Scene Solving- The Participants are split into teams of 2. The host shows a picture revolving around a crime scene. Clues are given. The teams are required to solve the mystery. Top 3 teams qualify for the next round

Round 4
Kahoot Trivia Quiz- In this round, the participants play a quiz as a team and the scores are calculated. The teamwith the highest score calculated cumulatively in round 3 and round 4 wins.

Paper Presentation

A paper presentation event is a technical event that provides the participants with a platform to showcase their research ideas in front of an esteemed panel of judges/experienced academicians/researchers.

Event type: Technical
Participation: Teams of 1-3, Any department
Platform: Google Meet
No. of Rounds: 2
Date/Time: Jan 23rd, 9am to 1pm



Shortlisting Criteria/Paper Evaluation:
The papers received from the participants will be evaluated by the coordinators to check if they are in accordance with the specified format of the paper (IEEE standards). The papers will subsequently be checked for plagiarism by means of plagiarism detectors available online. The papers which are not plagiarized and in accordance with the specified format will then be evaluated based on innovation scope and feasibility. The final shortlist count will be based on the time allocated for the event.


Description:

The interested participants will be asked to submit their papers via email (to krijeshan17078@cse.ssn.edu.in) The received papers would then be split accordingly among event coordinators to validate them against the shortlisting criteria specified above. The shortlisted participants will then be contacted via email by the event head to confirm their selection. They will then be asked to prepare a PPT presentation for their research topic and submit the same two or three days prior to the event to the event head via email. (Non-submission of PPT in time will also lead to elimination) The shortlisted participants will then be shared GMeet links to the event. During the GMeet call, they would be presenting the prepared PPT before the audience(other shortlisted participants) and the panel of judges(yet to be decided) in turns and will be on call till the event concludes. The decision of the panel will remain final and accordingly the winner will be declared.
