Papyrus

Have a Research/Review Paper? Come present it with us at "Papyrus", our Paper Presentation Event

Event type: Technical
Participation: Teams of 1-3, Any department
Platform: Zoom
No. of Rounds: 2
Date/Time: Jan 23rd, 9am to 1pm


Round 1
In the first round, participants form a team of upto 3 members and submit their paper and abstract via mail. The papers are then shortlisted and the selected teams are notified.

Round 2
 In the second and final round, shortlisted teams are lined up. Each team presents their paper for 7 minutes followed by a Q&A session for 2 minutes. The top 3 papers will be selected as winners.


Rules:
- Each paper can have upto 3 authors.
- Submitted papers must be in IEEE format.
- Papers must not be published before anywhere or be under consideration for publication in any journal or conference.
- Plagiarism is not allowed. Original works only.
- Paper length limited to 6 pages.
- Paper must be in .pdf, .docx, or .doc formats.
- A team can have participants from different institutions.
- The paper is to be attached to the mail paperpresentationinvente2020@gmail.com
with the subject as the title of the paper.
- The mail must contain - name of authors, college names and departments, phone numbers, and email addresses.
