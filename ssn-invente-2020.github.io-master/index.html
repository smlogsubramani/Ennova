About 

INVENTE, the annual technical fest of SSN Institutions is an event which etched its name in
the walls of the most familiar and awaited college festivals. The 5th Edition of INVENTE, albeit
online, is no different from the editions of the past and aims to showcase the technological
prowess of students across the country.
